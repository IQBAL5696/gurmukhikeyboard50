Privacy Policy for Gurmukhi Keyboard

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Gurmukhi Keyboard respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

1. Information We Collect

Microphone Access
The app may request access to the microphone for voice-to-text input. Audio is processed in real time by the speech recognition service available on your device. The app itself does not store or record audio data.

Keyboard Input
Text typed using the keyboard is used only for providing typing functionality and suggestions. The app does not store or transmit what you type.

Nanakshahi Calendar Feature
The app includes a Nanakshahi Calendar for viewing Sikh dates and events. All calendar calculations are processed locally on the device. No calendar usage data is collected or shared.

AI Features
The app may use on-device machine learning features to provide typing suggestions, translations, or text recognition. These processes are performed locally on your device and are not used for training models by the developer.

2. How We Use Information

Any information processed by the app is used only to provide its core functionality such as keyboard typing, voice input, calendar viewing, and smart suggestions. We do not sell, trade, or share personal information with third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

Some features may rely on services provided by your device or operating system (such as speech recognition). These services may have their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage

The app does not store personal information on external servers. Most processing occurs locally on your device.

5. User Consent

By using Gurmukhi Keyboard, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
