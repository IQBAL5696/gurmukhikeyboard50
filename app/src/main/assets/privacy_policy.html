Privacy Policy for Gurmukhi Keyboard

Last updated: January 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Gurmukhi Keyboard may collect or access the following information:

  • Microphone Access: The app uses the microphone for voice-to-text input. Audio data is processed in real-time and is not stored or transmitted, except to the respective speech recognition services you have enabled on your device.
  • Nanakshahi Calendar: The app includes a Nanakshahi Calendar feature for viewing dates and religious events. All calendar data and calculations are processed locally on your device. No information related to your calendar usage or event viewing is collected or shared.
  • AI Features: The app uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) to provide smart typing suggestions, translations, and text recognition. Most of these features use on-device processing via Google ML Kit. Any text processed by AI is used solely to provide the requested functionality and is not stored or used by us for training purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used solely to provide and improve the app's features. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.
